India's Flavours

India is a land of 29 states, each carrying its own culinary heritage — its own spices, techniques, and stories passed through generations. We bring all of them to your table in the heart of York, opposite the historic Clifford's Tower.

At 29 States, every dish is crafted with authentic ingredients and time-honoured recipes, served with the warmth and hospitality that defines Indian culture. From rich Mughlai gravies to coastal seafood specialties — every plate is a voyage.

Our Heritage

Embracing the Timeless Flavors of India

When we first opened our doors, India was home to 29 states, each with its unique flavours and traditions. Although India now comprises 28 states, the essence of our cuisine remains unchanged.

Our restaurant's name, inspired by the 29 states, serves as a testament to the rich culinary heritage of India. Our chefs have carefully crafted a menu that weaves together the bold flavours, vibrant spices, and aromatic ingredients from across the country.

At The Indian Kitchen, York, we invite you to savour the timeless appeal of Indian cuisine. Our dishes will transport you on a culinary journey across the country. Join us, and let the aromas and flavours of India captivate your senses.
